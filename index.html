About The National Consultants

Welcome to The National Consultants. I provide custom, advisory services to Taylor Swift fans who want to learn more about The National, one of the iconic rock groups of the 21st century.

Why we exist

In 2020, legendary pop musician Taylor Swift released two 'surprise' albums: Folkore and Evermore .

Both albums featured songs produced 'in secrecy' by Aaron Dessner, composer and multi-instrumentalist for The National. For many Swifties, the work of The National may have been previously unknown, and I want to help them explore their rich discography.

My offerings

To help explorers get started, my website features:
  • A primer about The National
  • Recommendations about albums and merchandise
I also offer personal coaching to explore the merits of specific National albums, songs, collaborations and their relevance to the Taylor Swift extended universe. These perspectives are worth paying significant dollars for because what is life without a deep devotion to The National.

Inquire with james.chang@kellogg.northwestern.edu for hourly rates for individuals or groups.

About me

My name is James Chang, and when I am not a full-time MBA student at the Kellogg School of Management, I am a full-time student of The National. I also stan Taylor Swift, whose bandwagon I joined after her 1989 album.

The collision of these two interests absolutely blew my mind and was one of the few bright spots in the 2020 cultural conversation. The National Consultants provide the services you need to navigate that conversation with confidence and precision.
