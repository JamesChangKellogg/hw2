About The National

From left to right: Bryce, Scott, Matt, Bryan, and Aaron
The National is a quintet of Cincinnati, OH natives who were living in NYC in the 2000s. Since 2001, The National have released 8 studio albums, which have garnered increasing critical acclaim. Their breakthrough album Alligator was released in 2005, one year before Taylor Swift's self-titled debut.
The music of the national is regularly described as 'melancholic,' 'anxious,' and 'existential'- adjectives that you would not regularly ascribe to Taylor Swift. The Atlantic recently described them as "arguably the band of this millennium to most consistently balance gut impact and brainy appeal, usually garnish their wallowing with a dignified strut." Therefore, it can come as somewhat of a suprise that Taylor considers them her "favorite band."
Fun Fact: The National features two pairs of twins: the Dessners (Aaron & Bryce) and the Devendorfs (Bryan & Scott).
